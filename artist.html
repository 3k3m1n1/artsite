Meet the Artist

Ekemini Nkanta

Hi! I'm a self-taught artist and front-end developer thriving in New York City :) I’ve been drawing since I could hold a pencil, but didn’t start taking it seriously until I launched my own business in January. Painting, writing and coding are all major parts of who I am, and I really hope one of them takes me far someday. So far, I've won 3rd place in my high school's talent show for performing slam poetry, and was recently announced a 2018 National Gold Medalist in the Scholastic Art and Writing Awards.

The Meaning Of Meraki

"Meraki" is a Greek word that doesn't translate smoothly to any noun in English. The best way to describe it is when one does something with soul, creativity, or love; or places a part of themselves into their work.
